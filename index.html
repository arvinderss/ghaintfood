    Our Story

    Ghaint — Punjabi slang for something outstanding, bold, straight-up superb. That's the standard we hold every plate to.

    Ghaint Food started in Ludhiana with a simple idea: street-style fast food doesn't have to cut corners, and home-style food doesn't have to be boring. So we do both — cheesy, loaded, made-to-order burgers, pizzas and fries for when you want indulgence, and honest, home-cooked tiffins for when you just want a proper meal.

    Everything's cooked fresh to order, packed to travel well, and priced fairly for takeaway — order direct on WhatsApp and pay what we actually charge, no platform markup.
