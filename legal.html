How we keep your information safe

We built this site to need as little of your personal information as possible. In plain terms:

  • We don't store your personal data on this website. There is no customer account system and no customer database behind this site — so there is no store of personal information here to be lost, leaked or stolen.
  • Your connection is encrypted. The site is served only over a secure (HTTPS) connection, so information moving between your browser and the site is encrypted in transit.
  • No tracking, no advertising. We don't use advertising, analytics or third-party tracking. The only things we save on your device are small preference settings — your chosen language, light/dark theme, and whether food photos are shown — which don't identify you.
  • We never see your payment details. Payments are made through your own UPI app. We never receive or store your card number, bank details or UPI PIN.
  • The pages are hardened. The site uses a strict Content-Security-Policy and other browser safeguards to reduce the risk of tampering or malicious code, and the preference settings above are stored with secure cookie flags.
  • We only use what you send us. When you place an order over WhatsApp, we use the details in your message — your name, phone number, order, and delivery address if you choose delivery — only to prepare and hand over or deliver that order.

No online system can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but because we deliberately collect and keep so little, there is very little about you for anyone to obtain from this site.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ghaint Food (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use this website and order from us.

1. Our privacy-first approach

This website is a menu and an order-builder. It has no login, no shopping account and no server that stores customer records. Your cart is held only in your browser while you use the page and is cleared when you reload or close it. When you're ready, the site helps you send your order to us as a WhatsApp message — nothing about your order is saved on the website itself.

2. Information we collect

We only receive the information you choose to send us when you place an order, which typically includes:

  • your name and WhatsApp phone number (these reach us through WhatsApp when you message us);
  • the items in your order and your chosen pickup or delivery option;
  • your delivery address, only if you select delivery and provide it.

We do not collect this through a form on the site — it comes to us inside your WhatsApp message. We do not ask for or receive your payment card, bank account or UPI PIN at any point.

3. Cookies and local settings

We use a small number of “strictly functional” cookies to remember your display preferences: your selected language, light or dark theme, and whether food photos are shown. These are not used to track you, are not shared with anyone, and contain no personal information. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

4. How we use your information

We use the order details you send only to:

  • prepare your order correctly;
  • arrange pickup or delivery;
  • contact you about that order (for example, to confirm it or ask a question); and
  • keep a basic record of the sale for our own accounts.

We do not sell, rent or trade your information, and we do not use it for marketing without your agreement.

5. Who your information reaches

  • WhatsApp / Meta: your order message travels over WhatsApp, which is operated by Meta under its own terms and privacy policy.
  • Your UPI app / bank: if you pay by UPI, the payment is handled between your UPI app and your bank. We only receive a confirmation from you (such as a screenshot).
  • Delivery: if you choose delivery, whoever delivers your order will be given your address so they can bring it to you.

6. How long we keep it

We keep order messages and any basic sales record only for as long as we reasonably need them — to complete your order, handle any follow-up, and meet ordinary bookkeeping needs — and then we remove them. You can ask us to delete your WhatsApp conversation with us at any time.

7. Your rights

You can ask us to show you what order information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 gives you rights over your personal data, including access, correction and erasure, and the ability to raise a grievance. To exercise any of these, contact us using the details in section 9.

8. Children

This site is intended for general audiences ordering food and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly seek personal information from children.
